Ring & Balls

It is a 3D graphic that emphasizes visuals. It is often referred to as 'abstract art'. Both the material and modeling are simple, but the combination of mild-feeling colors gave me a sense of stability. In addition, I emphasized abstract feeling rather than realistic feeling by using setting that reflects camera perspective and indirect lighting.
